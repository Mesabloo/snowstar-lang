A little history about it:
Snow* is born out of ashes and pain in 2018. The first version of the "Assembler" was made in 1 month. The objective behind it was to prove that I, Mesabloo, was able to create my own language, as I always was dreaming about. Finally this idea came to my mind and the language got created after that, under an ASM-like syntax.
Description of the language:
Snow* is: Statically typed: This means that anything you write is checked during compile time and not runtime, what makes the VM a bit faster to boot up.Weakly typed: The language has no explicit type specification. you can use a string mostly in the same exact context as a number. Pure shit: Despite the fact that the syntax is pretty much self-explanatory, the way it works is sometimes really abstract, and it might also be totally confusing.
